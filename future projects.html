Description

This is a music player that the user places a chip with a QR code. A RFID scanner will scan the chip like how the public uses contactless transactions with their bank card. The Raspberry Pi will take the scanner's input and play the appropriate song using a Spotify API to play the music. The Raspberry Pi will then connect the wireless earbuds (that were going to be thrown away) which will play the music with audio amplication with speaker drivers.

This video sparked my interest into making a music player. The creator uses wireless earbuds that were being sold for parts, he made an easy restore, and it was a process of making the right connections with the earbuds to the speaker drivers. I chose this method as earbuds today pack a lot of tech inside, and to reuse the tech into my project is what I wanted to add to my project. LINK: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNHDbXAmY_0

Description

This drone will act as an automated delivery system. The package will tell the drone if the weight, and the drone will take in drag resistance. With those two factors it will determine if the drone can carry the package through an algorithm. When the drone decides to take the package it will track and align itself with the package's location. Once at a close range it will decent avoiding obsticles. Once decended close to the package range it will make micro adjustments and align the carriage's magnets with the electoactivated magnets in the drone for immediate connection. Once connected the permenent locking mechanism will activate, and the drone will be on it's way.
