Hey, My name is Danish Nadeem pronounced /Dan ish/ /Na deem/. I was born and raised in Canada all my life. Ever since I was little I had a great intrest in making stuff, so when it came time to go to University I decided to go to Ontario Tech University because they offered a Mechatronics Program and had a great Co-op program to allow me to network with other industries to have access to great opportunities. I am hoping that YOU will have an intrest in working together in the near future. ;)

When it came time to choose a program for my post secondary I wanted to be in a program that is versitile and does not pose limits to what I want to learn. I came to learn about mechatronics engineering a few years prior, and fell in love with it as it a combination of mechanical, electronic, and software engineering. I was also happy to learn that the program is offered by a univeristy close enough that I would not need residence, hence is the reason why am I enrolled in the MECHATRONICS ENGINEERING program, at Ontario Tech Univeristy.

I am studying mechatronics engineering which is a field in engineering where the combination of mechanical, electronic, and computer science is used. The program give me experience from each of those fields which I can use in many applications, such as robotics, mechanical and electronic systems.
